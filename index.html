What's so great nowadays?

The news likes to advertise negative actions of others, but if we look closer, there is actually a lot of service going on as well. There are people making a difference locally as well as globally in good ways!

Aside from the new advancements in technology we see every day, we have also made advancements in protecting the environment. We aren't perfect at it, but we're much better than we used to be, and we have much natural beauty to enjoy for it!

One bright thing about the current time is the rising generation. Yes, they face a lot of challenges, but these challenges are turning them into some of the smartest and strongest individuals our world has seen!
